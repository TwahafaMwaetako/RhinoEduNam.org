What is Rhino Poaching?

Rhino poaching is the illegal hunting of rhinos for their horns, which are highly valued in some cultures for their supposed medicinal properties. Rhino poaching is a serious problem in many parts of the world, including Etosha National Park.

The Importance of Rhinos

Rhinos are an important part of the ecosystem and play a crucial role in maintaining the balance of nature. They are also a keystone species, which means that their presence or absence can have a significant impact on the environment.
